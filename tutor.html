Tutoring Service

Here at Study at Carolina, we are dedicated to helping students academically in a wide variety of subjects from calculus and chemistry to writing and history free of charge. Our services offer one-on-one peer-led tutoring during chosen hours with the option for meetings to be in-person or online to allow students more flexibility in their already busy schedule. In addition to one-on-one meettings we also offer workshops during exam season to help better prepare students during the final stretch of the semester. Our service strives to help take students the gain the knowledge and expertise they need they need during their time here at UNC Chapel Hill!
